Department OF COMPUTER APPLICATION

The Department was established in 2003 and is one of the most reputed academic departments in the field of Computer Application. The curriculum of this department is addressed to the students who wish to be distinguished as Software Developer, Computer System Analyst, Software Engineer, Software Application Architect, Hardware Engineer, Software Consultant, Database Administrator in the private and public sector. You can become part of this highly competitive and futuristic environment by enrolling for one of our exciting programs.
